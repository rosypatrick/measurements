Body Measurement Capture

Instructions

This app helps you capture body measurements using your camera. Follow these steps:

  1. Allow camera access when prompted
  2. Stand 2-3 meters away from the camera
  3. Wear fitting clothes for accurate measurements
  4. Use the reference object for scale calibration
  5. Capture front and side views
  6. Review and export your measurements
